Our choice of technology

Substrate is the perfect framework for us to build our protocol on. It allows us full control over our runtime and the ability to customize it to our needs. Furthermore it offers a lot of standardized building blocks in terms of governance,asset standards and interoperability we can use in the future.

This is ideal for us to further refine our protocol into a decentralized housing protocol. You can find more about our current development progress in our Github Repo.
